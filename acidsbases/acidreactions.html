You might be wondering now, what happens when an acid and base react with each other?

The reaction between an acid and a base is actually called a neutralisation reaction. In this reaction the acid and base react to form a salt and water.

The hydrogen ions from the acid and the hydroxide ions from the base, when present in exactly the same amount will neutralize each other and form water and an ionic salt.

NaOH is actually a metallic oxide. Infact, all metallic oxides are said to be basic oxides.
