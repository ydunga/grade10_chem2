Carbon reacts with oxygen to give off carbon di-oxide and releases heat and light in the process.

Saturated hydrocarbons usually give off a clear flame while unsaturated hydrocarbons give off a yellow flame.

Fuels like petroleum and coal contain small amounts of nitrogen and sulphur in them. During combustion these elements form oxides of sulphur and nitrogen which are very polluting for the environment.
