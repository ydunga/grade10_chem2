Transition metals

Tranisition metals are all metals placed between group 3 and 13 in the periodic table.

Transition metals can also conduct electricity, but they have higher melting points and higher densities than the metals in group 1 and 2.

Transition metals are also more electronegative than group 1 or group 2 metals.

Transition metals react very slowly or not at all with oxygen and water at room temperature.
