Oxidation and Reduction

Oxidation is the process of loss of electrons/ gain of oxygen by a substance.

Oxygen was one of the first identified oxidizing agents. There are several other oxidizing agents. Elements belonging to the halogen group are also oxidizing agents!

You can think of an oxidizing agent as a substance that accepts electrons.
